Baking Mail채nderli cookies (Milanese cookies)

Recipe

  • 250g butter
  • 225g sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 5 raw eggs
  • 1 lemon peel
  • 500g flour
  • Water

Prepare Mail채nderli cookies (Milanese cookies) dough

First place the butter, salt and sugar in a bowl and mix it all together. then add 3 raw eggs and continue to mix the mixture until it has taken a lighter color. Add the lemon peel and the flour. Mix the mixture until it becomes a whole, then let it chill for at lest 2 hours.

Shape cookies

Take the Milanese dough out of the fridge about 15 minutes before rolling it out. Roll the dough on a little flour and then cut the dough into any shape you like. Just before baking in the oven, chill the cookies for anoher 15 minutes.

Brush the Milanese

Mix 2 raw eggs with the Water and coat the Mail채nderli cookies (Milanese cookies) with it. You can draw fine patterns with a fork if you like.
